Thank you for using GloboNote! You can use GloboNote to create sticky notes, to-do lists, reminders, personal journal and things you need to remember.

Basic guide on how to use GloboNote:

* Create new notes

Double click on icon in the system tray or Right click on icon in the system tray then click New Note

* Move note around

On editing area, press and hold Alt+Left mouse click then move your mouse or on title bar, hold Left mouse click then move your mouse

* Resize note

Move your mouse to note's edges and drag it

* Change note preferences

Right click on note then click Preference

* Assign note to a Group

Right click on note then go to Assign to Group

* Delete note

Ctrl-F4 or Right click on note then click Delete

* Change note style using hotkey

Assign hotkey to note style using Note Preferences (Alt-Enter). Press Alt-1 to 8 to change note style.

* Detect hyperlink

Hold down Ctrl then move your mouse (e.g. http://sourceforge.net/projects/globonote/)
